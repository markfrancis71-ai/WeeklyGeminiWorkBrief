AI Proliferation, Token Guardrails & Standardized Gateways

The "Siren Song" of Third-Party AI Platforms

  • Restrictive License Models: Platforms like Salesforce are monetizing AI features via credit/token systems, leading to extreme pricing spikes and data "ingress/egress" tolls.
  • Copilot Resource Exhaustion: Heavy general-productivity usage inadvertently led to token exhaust. Access is being restricted to specialized technical roles (Data Scientists, Developers, Analysts) while exploring local LLMs on corporate hardware.
  • API & Schema Mapping: Initiating full-scale API and Avro Schema analysis to programmatically identify automation potentials and catalog corporate integrations.

"Do not let the AI siren song draw us into these platforms... continue to use the platforms for what they are good at and keep AI decoupled."
